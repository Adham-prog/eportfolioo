Course Activities

Activity 1: Rizal’s Tripartite View of History

Date: April 30

Description: Discussion of Rizal’s historical framework in three stages: the precolonial, colonial, and post-colonial periods.

Activity 2: Rizal and Education

Date: April 30

Description: Explored Rizal’s advocacy for education as a tool for national progress and reform.

Activity 3: The Role of Youth

Date: April 7

Description: Reflected on Rizal’s famous statement “The youth is the hope of the fatherland” and its relevance today.

Activity 4: The Role of Women in Nation-Building

Date: April 7

Description: Analysis of Rizal’s “Letter to the Women of Malolos” and the historical role of women in the nationalist movement.

Activity 5: Leadership

Date: April 7

Description: Examined Rizal’s qualities as a leader and how they can be applied to modern leadership principles.

Activity 6: The Sacrifice and Martyrdom of Rizal

Date: (Insert actual date)

Description: A group presentation on Dr. José Rizal's life, sacrifice, and execution in Bagumbayan.

Activity 7: Rizal and the Philippine Revolution of 1896

Description: A critical examination of Rizal’s views on the 1896 Philippine Revolution and his connection with the Katipunan.
